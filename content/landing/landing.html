About Gathable

Made by students struggling to quickly arrange gatherings due to the chaotically different timetables of everyone.

Made with the hope that students can painlessly figure out when everyone is free instead of arguring in group chats for ages.

Features of Gathable

Create your own personal timetable to keep track of your events this week.

Create groups with other people to share everyone's timetables and easily discover free time slots.
